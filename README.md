# Python-Poetry

poem = """
He walked in young, too young they’d say,
Before his first drink, before his day,
Into halls of hidden intelligence,
Where silence spoke with consequence.

Through guarded doors that opened wide,
With coded truths held deep inside,
No badge of age, no seasoned face,
Yet none disturbed his steady pace.

For youth itself became his key—
A quiet form of secrecy,
He didn’t know what he had done,
What he’d become by being young.

Among the minds that watched and knew,
He passed as someone passing through,
Unaware, yet standing there—
That age alone had led him there.
"""

# Print the poem
print(poem)
